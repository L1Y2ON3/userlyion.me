THOMAS MBOYA

Founder of the repulic of kenya

BIOGRAPHY

Thomas mboya was a kenyan trade unionist,independence activist,pan africanist and socialist.He was among the founding fathers of the republic of kenya. He led the negotiations for independence at the lancaster house confrence and was pivotal in the foundation of KANU {Kenya African National Union} where he was the secretary general.He laid foundations for kenya capitalists and mixed economy at peak time of world war and set up several kenyan labour instituions.Thomas was the minister of economic planning and developmennt at the time when he was later assainated.
